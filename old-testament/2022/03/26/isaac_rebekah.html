Isaac and Rebekah: The Covenant Renewed

The Covenant Renewed: Genesis 24:27

Warm-up finding Rebekah

Warm-Up activity, abbreviated review of Servant finding Rebekah, wife for Isaac. As individual or in groups of 2 or 3 review this story of finding Rebekah. Think and enumerate the qualities of Rebekah. Think of characteristics that you believe demonstrates qualities that can help a person create happiness? Qualities in marriage—and other aspects of life? Add to that qualities you see in the servant.

  1. Servant call to go to land of Abraham: Genesis 24:2
  2. Servant takes camels and goods to Mesopotamia, Nahor: Genesis 24:10
  3. Rebekah an answer to Servant prayers: Genesis 24:42-48
  4. Rebekah goes with Servant and is blessed: Genesis 24:58-60
  5. Isaac and Rebekah meet: Genesis 24:63-67
  6. Continue reading through Abrahamic Covenant renewed.

Pottage

We can choose between immediate gratification and things of greater value. Jacob acquires birth right from Esau.

Genesis 25

29 ¶ And Jacob sod pottage: and Esau came from the field, and he was faint:

30 And Esau said to Jacob, Feed me, I pray thee, with that same red pottage; for I am faint: therefore was his name called Edom.

31 And Jacob said, Sell me this day thy birthright.

32 And Esau said, Behold, I am at the point to die: and what profit shall this birthright do to me?

33 And Jacob said, Swear to me this day; and he sware unto him: and he sold his birthright unto Jacob.

34 Then Jacob gave Esau bread and pottage of lentils; and he did eat and drink, and rose up, and went his way: thus Esau despised his birthright.
